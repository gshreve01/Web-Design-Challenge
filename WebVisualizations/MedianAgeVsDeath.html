A Study of Covid-19

Based on a Data Analytics project covering the counties of North Carolina

Median Age VS Number Of Deaths

This graph shows a slightly negative correlation between median ago of a county and the number of deaths indicating that there is a greater chance to die if you are young than if you are old. This is counter from what has been publicly stated at the time. This is another indication that the aggregate age of a county is not necessarily a good indicator of those in the county that are getting sick and dying. The statistical values calculated are:

R value: -0.2994
R*R value: 0.08964
P value: 0.003552
