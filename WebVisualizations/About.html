A Study of Covid-19

Based on a Data Analytics project covering the counties of North Carolina

The Goals of the Project

  • Use Census data to establish correlation with demographics and Covid 19 data
  • The top 10 counties that got affected with Covid 19
  • Understand the correlation between the following:

    • Population Vs Confirmed cases
    • Median Age Vs Confirmed cases
    • Median age Vs Death
    • Household Income Vs Confirmed cases
  • Develop Heatmap for Confirmed & Death cases in North Corolina by county
