Summary

A Bootcamp Trilogy Project

A study of the Covid 19 virus during the month of April 2020 using demographic census data. The attempt was to discover if any correlations existed between geographic populations, age, and economic factors. The data was gathered using APIs provided for Covid 19 and the cencus data for the counties of North Caralina. This data was converted into Pandas data frames to gather statistical information, and generate various charts. The conculsion was that the county population data was useful for analysis, while median age and median income of were probably not significant enough to analyze the Covid 19 data.
